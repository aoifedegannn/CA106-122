Competitions

Gymnastics has been contested in the Summer Olympics since the very first competition back in 1896. For some amount of years only men were permitted to compete. Women first began competing in 1928 in Amsterdam in the Artistic Events. In 1984 Rythmitic Events were introduced and in 2000 Trampoline Events were introduced to the competition.
