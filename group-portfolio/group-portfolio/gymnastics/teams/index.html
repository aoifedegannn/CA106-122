Teams

USA's women's gymnastics team is currently the reigning World team champion and reigning Olympic World team champion. In 2012 the team included Gabby Douglas, the first woman of colour to win the Olympic individual all-round. The team was given the nickname the 'Fierce Five'. The 2016 team, featuring Simon Biles was nicknamed the 'Final Five'.

The Team GB men's gymnastic team is made up of Max Whitlock, Joe Fraser, James Hall, Giarnni Regini-Moran and Dom Cunningham. The team recently placed 5th in the Artistic Gymnastics World Championships in Stuttgart, Germany.
