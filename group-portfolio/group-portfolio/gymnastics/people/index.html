Sports People

Simon Biles is currently the highest ranked female gymnast in history. She was born in Ohio on the 14th of March 1997 and first tried gymnastics when she was just 6 years old. She currently has 19 world gold medals and a combined total of 30 Olympics and World Championship medals making her the most decorated American gymnast. Biles is often referred to as the greatest gymnast of all time.

Max Whitlock is a British Gymnast who was born on the 13th of January in 1993. He rose to fame in 2012 when he won a bronze medal for the pommel horse at the London Olympic games. He now has five Olympic medals; two gold and three bronze. Whitlock is Great Britain's most successful Olympic Gymnast and first ever Olympic champion in gymnastics.
