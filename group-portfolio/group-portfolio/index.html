Welcome!

This website was made by Aoife Deegan, Darragh Brennan, Davin Ayyubov, Ciaran Farrell and Turlough Duignan. We are all students at DCU. Aoife and Davin are studying Computer Applications while Darragh, Turlough and Ciaran are studying Enterprise Computing. As a group we all decided we have a common interest in sport and together we realised the importance of sports in our everyday lives, because of this we were interested in creating a website to encourage those of you who are intrigued in sports, but aren't quiet sure if you should give it a go yourself. Each of us chose a sport of great interest to us and wrote up some information regarding that sport. Please feel free to browse through our website and don't be afraid to get in touch with us by clicking here.
